Webinar: Proactively Managing Your Employee Relations in Today's Workplace 
A Demonstration & Discussion with Deb Muller, CEO of HR Acuity

Wednesday, July 10, 11 a.m. to 12 p.m. EDT

 

From managing mental health to creating cultures of transparency to conducting best-practice investigations, has there ever been a more challenging time in employee relations?

It’s hard – but it’s also a special mission to build safer workplaces.

How can technology help? Join HR Acuity CEO and employee relations expert Deb Muller as she reviews the latest trends in employee relations and how you can build a proactive program, enabled by technology.

Deb will take your questions as well as provide a demonstration of HR Acuity’s award-winning platform. Save your spot today!

Details:

- We will be using GoToWebinar.

- You can join our session by using a Mac, PC or a mobile device.

 

Deb Muller is the CEO of HR Acuity, a technology solution that combines documentation, process, and human expertise so organizations can meet the challenge of managing employee relations in the modern world. Be proactive. Manage risk. Create a safer workplace.
