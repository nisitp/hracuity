The HR Acuity® Employee Relations Roundtable

Share best practices in employee relations, discuss the latest trends and challenges, and network with HR leaders.

 

The HR Acuity Employee Relations Roundtable brings together HR practitioners from around the country for a day and a half of interactive sessions on today’s toughest employee relations challenges.

The invitation-only Roundtable presents a unique opportunity for dialogue and discussion of best practices in employee relations. Attendees return to their organizations energized with valuable knowledge and ready to put into practice new ideas within their teams.

Here’s what to expect:

  • Innovative ideas and helpful strategies to improve your practices
  • A fresh perspective gained through shared knowledge from peers across the industry
  • Insights into trends and challenges unique to employee relations
  • A growing network of employee relations professionals to continue the conversation year-round

2019 Roundtable Agenda
  • HR Acuity Client Pre-session (new this year!): A special pre-event meeting exclusive for HR Acuity clients will include:  a first look at new product features, an interactive session on calculating the ROI of ER, a chance to provide enhancement ideas and feedback to the HR Acuity leadership and product team plus a networking lunch.

  • #METOO A YEAR LATER: A look at the impact of the movement on employee relations including what companies have experienced, how they are responding and initiatives being implement to affect lasting change.

  • Employee Relations Documentation: What are the best (and most compliant) practices for capturing complete and consistent information on everyday issues, policy violations, investigation interviews and more?

  • Mental Health: Working with employees who are managing mental health issues is becoming more and more frequent.  This session will allow for an open discussion on your team's involvement, additional resource requirements, preparation for crisis management, effective methods of triage, and more.  
  • The Legal Perspective: Gain insights from both sides – an informative and lively discussion from a plaintiff attorney and a defending attorney
  • ER and Policies: Conflicting state and regional laws can make managing employee relations policies even more complex than usual.  Join the participants in discussion about what's worked and what hasn't.  Consider new ways to manage policies and practices as well as how to work amongst your functional groups in this session.  
  • The Power of Data: As organizations start to recognize employee relations as a strategic imperative, the demand for data becomes real. Learn and discuss how fellow roundtable participants are capturing and delivering data in an impactful way to their stakeholders.
  • “Hot Topic” Breakout Sessions: Extend the conversations.  We will use "parking lot" items from each formal session to create breakout sessions to allow the conversations to continue.  Shared summaries of these sessions will be made available so you won't feel as if you are missing out.  
  • Cocktails and Conversations: Keep on sharing and networking in a relaxed environment
